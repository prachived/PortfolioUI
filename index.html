My Journey

I'm Prachi Ved, a Software Developer.

As a kid I was always fascinated with the stories of Akbar-Birbal and Tenali Raman. What was so extraordinary in these stories was the way intelligence was merged with out of the box thinking to overthrow challenges. Same goes with coding, Machine Learning/Data Science is all about creating something useful for other people. There is no stop to learning. After all, what good is a thing if it involves monotony. It always interests me to learn more about system development. Due to this earnest curiosity, I took up engineering. To get insight into the field of Machine Learning, worked on a Voice Biometric system in the final year of college and also co-authored a technical paper based on the research done for the project. My skill sets flourished further by gaining Business and Industry experience by working at Deloitte as a Developer in SAP division and undertaking various projects based on SAP ABAP. At present, a student of Northeastern University pursuing a Masters degree in Computer Science with a focus on software development.
